Kiwi Compass

Find your place across the ditch

Moving to Australia is the easy part.
Picking the right city isn't.

Around 700,000 New Zealanders live across the ditch, and thousands more make the move every year. Most default to Sydney, Melbourne or wherever their cousins landed, but the city that suits a surfing sparkie is not the city that suits a gallery-going analyst who flies home every Christmas.

Kiwi Compass starts with who you are: your field of work, your hobbies, where you holiday, and the social life you want. It matches that against the reality of twelve Australian cities and gives you a ranked percentage match, plus the reasoning behind every score, so you can weigh it up yourself rather than trust a black box.

How the matching works

Each city is scored out of 100 across six weighted factors. Career fit carries the most weight, and two factors flex to match you: how much a Kiwi community matters, and how sensitive you are to living costs.

  • Career fitemployment strength for your field in that city
  • Lifestyle & hobbiesgeography, climate and venues for what you love doing
  • Social lifefit against the pace and scene you actually want
  • Kiwi communityNZ-born residents, as counted in the Census
  • Travel connectionsdirect flights to the places you holiday, including trips home
  • Affordabilitymedian rents, weighted by how much cost matters to you

Where the data comes from

Every factor in your results names its source. The city profiles are compiled from official Australian data:

  • ABS Census 2021 NZ-born population of each city (country of birth), driving the Kiwi-community factor
  • ABS labour statistics employment by industry and region, driving the career-fit factor
  • Bureau of Meteorology 30-year climate averages for temperature, rainfall and sunshine hours
  • Scheduled airline routes, 2025 direct international coverage from each city's airport, driving the travel factor

Honesty matters more than polish: the dataset is an indicative snapshot. Figures are rounded and compiled at a point in time, not fetched live. A future version will refresh them automatically from the ABS Data API. Use the results to shortlist and compare, then verify the numbers before you book the one-way ticket.
